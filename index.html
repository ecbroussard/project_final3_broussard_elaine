Events

Karaoke Thursdays

Avoid the smoke and commotion of karaoke bars but still get your weekly sing on. Join us every Thursday from 5 t 9 p.m. for a family friendly round of karaoke. Our experienced KJ Lizzy the Fizzy will keep the party going all night. Munch on your favorite Pizza Planet dishes while watching your friends and family sing their hearts out to the hottest tunes. Participate in our weekly karaoke content to win free coupons for Pizza Planet fare.

80s Arcade Nights

Join us on the second Tuesday of every month for 80s Arcade Night. Play your favorite vintage arcade games while jamming out to yesterday's hottest tunes. Our retro DJ Sam the Jam will keep the records spinning. Gobble down slices of your favorite Pizza Planet pies in between games. All attendees receive a free $5 arcade token credit. Participate in our monthly 80s costume competition to win a free ice cream sundae.

Your Own Special Event

Did you know you could have a birthday party or other special event at Pizza Planet? Our party room is available for private events, and the room is even free if your party orders enough food and drink! Plus the birthday boy or girl will receive a complimentary ice cream sundae or piece of cheesecake! The best parties happen here at Pizza Planet. Contact Lacie at 555-555-5555 to inquire about our party rental space.
